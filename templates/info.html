IMPORTANT NOTE:
Before you start driving your train model, you need to start the engine. When you start the engine, your 5 minutes of play time will start to countdown. Your play time will end if you switch off the engine or 5 minutes has passed from the engine start.

GENERAL INSTRUCTIONS:
Key Binds:

- W: Move the train forwards (counter-clockwise)
- S: Move the train backwards (clockwise)
- R: Rail Road Switch. When disabled, it goes straight. When enabled, it switches to another track.
- Space / E: Stop the train

Everything above can be controlled by mouse click or keybinds.
